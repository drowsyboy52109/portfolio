Career

A Physician-Scientist


Job Summary

Physician-Scientists spend a lot of time getting more knowledge about the medical field through research and experiments.


Salary and Education needed

Physician-Scientist make an average of $350,000 a year. To be one you need an MD-PhD which means a Doctorate of Medicine and of Philosophy degree.


Main Skills

The basic skills needed to be a Physician-Scientist are Compassion, Analysis, Communication, Communication skills, Critical thinking, Humility, Organization, Professional, Resilience.
